About StudLife

StudLife is a college based online portal (currently accustomed to IIIT Allahabad) which is aimed at making life easier and more productive by making day to day errands easier to complete. It was developed in March'20 by the students of IIIT-A with the purpose of encapsulating health center, canteen, warden office and locker for documents into one web based application. The model is very flexible and can be adjusted to meet the requirements of residential colleges across the country. {#

Why StudLife?

Life at IIIT-A, is busy and should be. As a team, we realised that the bright minds of the campus had to spend a huge chunk of their daily life catering to everyday requirements. And wasted a lot of time waiting for food to get ready, wait for a doctor and come back disappointed or run around to find the Warden inorder to get an outpass . This is where StudLife will come to one's rescue. With the click of a button one can book appointments, get outpasses approved and order food from the night canteen: all whilst doing something productive!
